about

Nice to meet you! I'm a first-generation undergraduate student in my third year at UC Berkeley studying Applied Mathematics with an emphasis in Computer Science.

Growing up in the art capital of the US, I found joy in pursuing visually creative projects, but I've never considered doing it professionally. Now, I'm reconsidering what defines artistry and want to explore it through technology. My interests include computer graphics, digital image processing, and computational geometry.

This summer, I’m developing playback and media tools as a Software Engineer Intern at Disney Animation on the Studio Tools team. When at school, I enjoy creating for the world’s largest collegiate hackathon, learning about the animation pipeline to create stories, and supporting the journeys of women and non-binary students in engineering.

Here to promote creativity and connection through interactive and visual media.
